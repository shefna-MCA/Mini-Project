Welcome to E-Bus Pass

The E-Bus Pass project for Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC provides a digital platform for generating bus concession cards. This initiative streamlines the process of obtaining travel passes by leveraging electronic systems. Users can access the platform to apply for, renew, and manage their bus concession cards online, reducing paperwork and administrative hassles. The system integrates with KSRTC databases to ensure accurate and efficient card generation. With a user-friendly interface, the project enhances the accessibility and convenience of public transportation, facilitating seamless travel experiences for commuters while embracing the benefits of digital technology.
